Cookie Policy

LAST UPDATED: MARCH 2026

At ZeroKey, we believe in keeping your digital footprint as small as possible. This policy explains exactly what cookies we use and why.

1. What We Do NOT Track

We do not use advertising cookies, marketing pixels, or third-party behavioral trackers. Your encryption keys, payload data, and personal browsing history remain entirely untouched by tracking scripts.

2. Google Analytics

The only cookies we set are for Google Analytics. We use this strictly to monitor aggregate, anonymized performance metrics to keep the servers running smoothly.

  • This tells us how many total visits the site receives and general geographic regions (e.g., country-level data).
  • It does not collect PII (Personally Identifiable Information) or link your IP address to the encrypted payloads passing through our API.

3. Managing Your Cookies

If you prefer to block all analytics, you are fully welcome to use ZeroKey with privacy extensions like uBlock Origin, Privacy Badger, or browser-level tracking protection (like Brave's shields). ZeroKey's cryptographic engine will continue to function flawlessly even if all cookies are blocked.
